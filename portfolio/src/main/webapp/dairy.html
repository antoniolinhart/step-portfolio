One of my interests is analytics and visualizing data, so I used the Google Charts and Maps API to display some interesting data about dairy products and the largest cattle farms in the United States which I found on the USDA website!

I am not personally fond of milk, but I thought it would be interesting to plot this data and potentaially find a trend following the 'Got Milk?' campaign that started in 1993. It does appear that skim milk consumption increased in the few years after, but the other kinds of milk consumption decreased. Also, in the year-over-year chart, it shows some pretty large changes in flavored whole milk between years which I found very interesting in comparison to other types of dairy products.

In the future, I am looking forward to doing more data analysis like this!
